Electricity Management App Showcase

A promotional video for a fictional application that visualizes household energy consumption. Edited with Adobe Premiere Pro, focusing on clean motion graphics and intuitive UX storytelling.

01

Project Overview

This project is a promotional showcase for a fictional app designed to help users track and optimize their electricity usage. The goal was to demonstrate the app's core value—simplifying complex data—through a dynamic yet clear visual narrative.

02

Background & Purpose

With rising energy costs being a global concern, I envisioned a solution that makes energy saving "visible" and "actionable." I created this video to practice advanced editing techniques and to showcase how a well-designed UI can be effectively communicated through motion.

03

Key Focus: Visual Clarity & Rhythm

Motion Graphics for UI Demonstration

  • Purpose: To guide the viewer's eye through the app's interface without overwhelming them with text, ensuring the "Ease of Use" is felt immediately.
  • Implementation: Using keyframe animation in Adobe Premiere Pro, I emphasized specific UI elements like the "Usage Dial" and "Monthly Comparison Graphs." Smooth transitions were synchronized with a rhythmic soundtrack to keep the viewer engaged.
04

Editing Tech Stack

05

Creative Commitment

User-Centric Storytelling

Every cut in this video was made with the user's perspective in mind. I avoided flashy effects that serve no purpose, choosing instead to focus on how the app solves the user's pain points—specifically the frustration of high utility bills.

Professional Polish

By meticulously adjusting the timing and easing of motion, I aimed for a professional level of polish that reflects the reliability and modern feel of the application itself.
