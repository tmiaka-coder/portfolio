Banner Design

Salt Bread Specialty Store
Opening Announcement

A banner design engineered to project expertise and brand authority, utilizing color theory-based visual strategies and deep target audience analysis.

01

Target Audience

The primary target audience is women in their 30s and 40s, including local residents and office workers. The composition appeals to quality-conscious consumers who value high-grade ingredients and artisanal craftsmanship.

02

Design Strategy

Visual Impact Using Complementary Colors

By adopting a blue background—a near-complementary color to the golden-brown (orange) of the bread—the main product is visually accentuated. This color scheme ensures the banner captures attention instantly.

Building Trust with Vertical Copy

Utilizing vertical catchphrases conveys the formality and reliability expected of a "specialty store." It subtly promotes themes of Japanese-sourced ingredients and professional skill through a sophisticated aesthetic.

03

Key Features

  • Used high-resolution product photography to maximize the texture and "sizzle" of the bread.
  • Achieved a sophisticated layout with ample white space while maintaining clear readability.
  • Designed for social media advertising, ensuring text remains legible even on smartphone displays.
