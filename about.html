Miaka Taniai（Vicky）

Aspiring Frontend Developer & QA Advocate

The Turning Point

After graduating from Nagasaki Junshin Catholic University, I started my career in the automotive industry, working in sales and customer service. Dealing with complex internal CRM systems daily, I often felt frustrated by recurring bugs and poor usability.

"I want to be the one who fixes these frustrations from the inside." This realization led me to transition into the IT industry, starting as a software tester for enterprise-level ERP systems.

Quality as a Foundation

In the world of QA (Quality Assurance), I developed a sharp eye for detail and a deep understanding of digital reliability. However, I soon realized that "testing after development" isn't enough.

I believe that high quality and accessibility should be woven into the code from the very first line. Combining my "Hospitality Mindset" from sales with my "Precision Mindset" from QA, I now focus on building websites that are inclusive by design.

2026: The New Chapter

I am currently preparing for my relocation to New Zealand in August 2026. My goal is to contribute to the NZ tech community by delivering honest, accessible, and legally compliant web solutions that bridge the gap between technology and people.
