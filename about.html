Identity

Vicky

Web Designer / Front-end Developer (QA Mindset)

After graduating from Nagasaki Junshin University, I began my career in the automotive industry, where I mastered the art of "Omotenashi" (hospitality) and user-centric communication through sales.

Transitioning into the IT sector, I served as a QA tester for enterprise ERP systems. This experience ingrained in me a rigorous standard for quality and a deep understanding of software reliability.

Today, I merge these two worlds: the empathetic perspective of service and the strict precision of a tester. I am committed to creating web experiences that are not only visually honest but also legally compliant and accessible to everyone, regardless of their environment.

Relocating to New Zealand in August 2026, I aim to contribute to global projects with a focus on web accessibility and sustainable frontend architecture.
